How it Works?

Search Bus

The "Search Bus" feature allows you to easily find buses by entering your departure location, destination, and travel date. Get instant results with schedules, fares, and booking options!

Pay

The "Pay" feature offers convenient payment options such as M-Pesa, Tigo Pesa, Airtel Money, T-Pesa, and mobile banking. Once payment is complete, you can receive your ticket via SMS, email, or download it directly.

Travel

The "Travel" feature makes your journey hassle-free. Simply present your SMS, downloaded, or printed ticket when boarding.
