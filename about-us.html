About Us

BusTicket is a Tanzania’s leading marketplace for bus tickets and management system. We offer services that enable bus operators and travelers to do everything online. Clients can get their travelling tickets while at home, offices and anywhere across the country.

We aim to revolutionize transportation industry by enabling bus operators to automate their company operational activities which in turn reduces their operational cost, save time, booking open for 24X7 and receive payment directly to their account once booking done.

Booking a bus ticket from anywhere made possible by us by just simply visit on to www.busticket.co.tz or booking through BusTicket App.


BusTicket customer service representatives are available 24x7 and happy to help your ticket booking process or attend to any of your issue.
